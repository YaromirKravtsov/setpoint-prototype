# Verwaltung des Testmonats durch den Schüler

**Type:** Flowchart — Student role
**File:** `Verwaltung des Testmonats durch den Schüler.png`

Student receives welcome email, sets password, logs in, and sees available trainings. They book sessions and can cancel (cancellation >24h before = slot stays available; <24h = slot is lost). After 4 completed trainings the system ends the trial month, blocks further bookings, and the admin contacts the student about continued participation.
